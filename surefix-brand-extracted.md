# Sure-Fix Remodeling Brand Guide — Extracted

## Colours
- French Blue: #394696 (primary — headlines, CTAs, buttons)
- Brown Red: #983631 (primary — emphasis, accents)
- Dusty Mauve: #7d8559 (secondary — sparingly)
- Alabaster Grey: #e9eae5 (secondary — backgrounds, dividers)
- White: #ffffff (backgrounds, long-form text areas)

## Typography
- Header/Display: Figtree (Google Fonts) — Bold weight preferred; always larger than Georgia
- Body: Georgia (Google Fonts) — Regular/Italic; always smaller than Figtree
- Combo: Bold Figtree + Light Figtree also recommended

## Logo Rules
- Primary: blue/red combination (preferred)
- No drop shadows, glows, or strokes on logo
- Never alter logo colours
- On dark backgrounds: use white version or white/red version
- On nav (dark bg): use white logo

## Quick Rules
- Primary colours for emphasis, NOT entire pages
- Neutral backgrounds (white or Alabaster Grey) for long-form text
- Avoid stacking multiple bold colours together
- Prioritize contrast for readability
- Use generous white space
- Clarity, consistency, simplicity

## Design Direction (Modern + Futuristic)
- Dark hero with video background
- Alabaster Grey (#e9eae5) sections for light areas
- French Blue (#394696) for section accents and CTAs
- Brown Red (#983631) for highlights and secondary CTAs
- Figtree Black/ExtraBold for large display headings
- Georgia for body copy paragraphs
