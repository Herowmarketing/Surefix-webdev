## Selected Approach: Approach 2 — Warm Modern Craft

This approach best serves Sure-Fix Remodeling's audience — homeowners in Easton, PA who want a trustworthy, premium contractor. The warm editorial aesthetic signals quality and professionalism without feeling cold or intimidating. The mascot becomes the friendly face of the brand, guiding visitors through the site.

**Design System Summary:**
- Background: `#f8f6f1` (warm off-white) for light sections, `#1a1f3e` (deep navy) for dark sections
- Primary: `#394696` (French Blue) for headers, nav, and key UI
- CTA: `#983631` (Brown Red) for all call-to-action buttons
- Accent: `#7d8559` (Dusty Mauve) for subtle decorative elements
- Text: `#1a1a2e` (near-black) on light, `#e9eae5` (alabaster) on dark
- Fonts: Figtree (Google Fonts) + Georgia
- Animations: Framer Motion, staggered fade-up, mascot bounce loop, stat counters
- 3D: Spline scene in hero background
